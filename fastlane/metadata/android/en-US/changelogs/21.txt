This release adds custom keys, more tmux controls, external auth, and machine sorting.

* Add your own text and control-key keys to the on-screen key row.
* Hold a key to repeat it, and tap a modifier to latch it for the next press.
* More tmux controls from the key row, including the prefix and menu.
* New External auth: let the SSH server authenticate you without saved credentials.
* Sort machines by last connected, name, or date added.
* Fixed extra spacing in the Appearance font preview.
